The International Business Program at Howard is one of the oldest in the US with its location in the nation's capital, which is home to many international organizations, NGOs, and embassies.

This proximity to a rich international environment provides students with ample experience of international exposure and international internship opportunities.

The International Business Program has two concentrations: Functional and Regional. Functional one includes Finance, Risk Management & Insurance, Marketing, Accounting, Management, Information Systems, Supply Chain Management, and so on; and Regional one covers Emerging Nations, Europe, Latin America, Middle East/North Africa, and Sub-Saharan Africa.
